About IT Day 2026

IT Day 2026 is the university's annual celebration of excellence in technology, research, and collaboration, bringing together university officials, faculty members, judges, guests, and students for a future-focused experience packed with competitions, performances, and major recognitions.

Event Highlights

From ceremonial traditions to high-energy competitions, exciting prize-worthy showdowns, and live entertainment, IT Day 2026 delivers two unforgettable days of talent, teamwork, and celebration. Tap any highlight for a quick sneak peek.

01

Grand Opening Must-See

Kick off the celebration with a Grand Parade featuring a brass band, followed by the Opening Ceremony, Flag Raising, and formal program proper.

02

Academic and Coding Arena

Dive into IT Quiz Bee, Fast Typing, and interactive booth activities, then take on programming challenges in Java, Python, SQL, and C#.

03

Esports Showdown Crowd Favorite

Feel the intensity of Mobile Legends from elimination rounds to tournament play, plus an action-packed Call of Duty Mobile tournament.

04

Sports and Fun Activities

Cheer for Men's Half Court Basketball and Women's Beach Volleyball, then join crowd-favorite activities like Parlor Games and Scavenger Hunt.

05

Mind-Challenging Games

Test strategy and speed in the Chess Tournament, Rubik's Cube Challenge, and Sudoku Challenge.

06

Main Stage Highlights Headliner

Experience the Musical Showcase: Battle of the Bands, then witness the headline event, Mr. and Ms. IT 2026.
