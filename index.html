About IT Day 2026

IT Day 2026 is the university's annual celebration of excellence in technology, research, and collaboration, bringing together university officials, faculty members, judges, guests, and students for a future-focused experience packed with competitions, performances, and major recognitions.

This year introduces an AI-inspired identity system where participating teams are grouped into distinct families - Claude, Grok, Gemini, and Dola - highlighting innovation, teamwork, and creative problem-solving throughout the celebration.

Event Highlights

From the opening parade to the major program, IT Day 2026 delivers two unforgettable days of talent, teamwork, and celebration. Tap any highlight to preview the same program blocks shown in the schedule.

01

Parade and Opening Program Must-See

Kick off the celebration with the Grand Parade, Opening Ceremony, Program Proper, Flag Raising, Invocation, MinSU Hymn, Opening Remarks, and competition announcements.

02

Competitions Begin

Dive into the Programming Competitions in Java, Python, C#, and SQL, plus Crimping, Fast Typing, Assembling and Disassembling, and Mobile Legends.

03

Esports Competition Crowd Favorite

Feel the intensity of Mobile Legends tournament play, plus an action-packed Call of Duty Mobile tournament.

04

Sports and Mind Games

Cheer for Men's Half Court Basketball and Beach Volleyball, then challenge yourself with Chess, Rubik's Cube, and Sudoku.

05

Continuation of Competitions

Follow the morning lineup with Ideathon Pitching, IT Quiz Bee, Digital Arts, and Call of Duty Mobile.

06

Major Program Headliner

Experience the major program with Mr. and Ms. IT 2026, Opening Proper, Battle of the Bands, Same Day Edit, Teaser Showing, and the Awarding Ceremony.
